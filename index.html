Phishing Email Simulation

This educational simulation demonstrates how phishing attacks trick users into revealing passwords. The simulation is completely safe and does not send or store any real data.


From: support-security@bank-alerts.com

Subject: Urgent! Your Account Will Be Suspended

Dear Customer,

We detected suspicious activity in your bank account. Please verify your account immediately by clicking the link below.

http://secure-bank-login.verify-user.com

Failure to verify within 24 hours may result in account suspension.

How to Identify Phishing Emails

  • Check suspicious sender email addresses.
  • Avoid clicking unknown links.
  • Look for urgent or threatening messages.
  • Verify website URLs carefully.
  • Use antivirus and email protection tools.
